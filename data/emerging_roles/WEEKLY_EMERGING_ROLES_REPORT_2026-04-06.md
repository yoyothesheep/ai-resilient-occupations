## Key Market Trends Identified

### Infrastructure Boom
Model Operations Engineer roles showing highest job posting volume (3,382-3,661) across all observed roles in the full research cycle. Clear market maturation and formalization of MLOps specialization.

### Design Specialization Emerging
AI Product Designer and Design Engineer (AI) represent dual-track specialization for design professionals entering AI. Clear skill differentiation from traditional design roles due to AI/ML fluency requirement.

### Role Differentiation by Responsibility
- **LLM Deployment Specialist:** Infrastructure focus (deployment, operations, optimization)
- **AI Product Designer:** User experience focus (interaction patterns, probabilistic outputs)
- **Prompt Experience Designer:** Content design focus (language, interaction frameworks)
- **Design Engineer (AI):** Technical implementation focus (component architecture, design systems)

### Salary Growth Trajectories
- **Entry-level:** LLM Ops ($185K avg), Design Engineer AI ($62K-$100K)
- **Mid-level:** AI SRE ($128K median), AI Product Designer ($150K-$250K)
