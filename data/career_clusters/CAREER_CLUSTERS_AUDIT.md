## Public Safety

### Law Enforcement (`law-enforcement`)

**Entry:** Police & Sheriff's Patrol Officers (33-3051.00) — HS diploma | police academy ~6mo | $76,290/yr

```
[L1] Police & Sheriff's Patrol Officers   33-3051.00  ★ entry
      │
      ├──→ [L1] Records/ID Officers         33-3021.02  specialization | forensic coursework | $500 | 3mo
      │                                                 (lower pay ceiling, not primary path)
      │
      ├──→ [L2] Detectives & Investigators  33-3021.00  ★ progression | 3yr exp | exam | $2k | 6mo
      │         │
      │         ├──→ [L2] Supervisors (Sgt) 33-1012.00  (less common from detective)
      │         │
      │         └──→ [L3] Private Detectives 33-9021.00  ★ lateral exit | PI license | $1k | 3mo
      │
      ├──→ [L2] First-Line Supervisors      33-1012.00  ★ progression | 5yr exp | sergeant exam | $2k | 6mo
      │                                                 (parallel track to detective)
      │
      ├──→ [L3] Private Detectives/PI       33-9021.00  lateral career exit | 5yr exp | $1k | 3mo
      │
      ├──→ [L2] Probation Officers          21-1092.00  cross-family lateral | bachelor's req | $30k | 4yr ✓ work
      │
      └──→ [Transit Police]  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────► (see below)
```

[L1] Transit and Railroad Police   33-3052.00  ★ entry
      │
      └──→ [Cross-family] Patrol Officers  33-3051.00  lateral | 2yr exp | $3k | 6mo
